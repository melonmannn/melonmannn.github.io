TF2R



What is this project?


TF2R is a completely remade version of Team Fortress 2, while still trying to preserve the old feel of it
It looks better, runs better, and supports almost all devices

Why should I play it over normal Tf2?


TF2R is more maintained then Valve's TF2, and may have even more content in the future
TF2R is also fairly moddable, since it can use TF2's models and animations without much effort

Can I support this project?


I am a nonprofit, and Valve probably wouldn't like me profitting off of this
